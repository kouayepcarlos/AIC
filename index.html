ABOUT ACI

Is an organization dedicated to promoting excellence in the field of Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) in Cameroon. Bringing together IT professionals, researchers and enthusiasts, the AIC aims to strengthen technical skills, encourage innovation and foster collaboration within the country's technology community.

Through a variety of initiatives, such as training courses, conferences and mentoring programs, the AIC works to develop its members and the IT sector, which is essential to the transformation of Cameroon. With a strong, modern visual identity, symbolized by its logo, AIC reflects its commitment to rigor, creativity and technological evolution. Together, we're building a prosperous digital future.

Our values at AIC

  • Excellence : AIC values professionalism, rigor and commitment in all its initiatives, encouraging its members to strive for excellence in their careers and projects.

  • Collaboration : AIC favors team spirit and knowledge sharing to build a supportive, clean IT community.

  • Innovation : At the heart of technological evolution, the AIC promises creativity and the exploration of new solutions to meet the challenges of the digital age.

  • Social commitment : AIC is committed to projects that make technology accessible to all, and contribute to the socio-economic development of Cameroon.

  • Ethics : Transparency, integrity and respect for ethical standards are the fundamental principles guiding all AIC actions.

Our AIC targets :

  • ICT professionals : engineers, developers, technicians.

  • Students : Young talents looking to enter the digital sector.

  • Companies : Need support in their digitalization process

  • Institutions : Public and private tech-related authors

  • Passionate : anyone interested in ICT.
